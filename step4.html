Step 4: Spellcasting Challenge

Harry, Ron, and Hermione are practicing their spells. Use logic to determine how many each cast.

"Three wizards—Harry, Hermione, and Ron—are practicing spells:
  • Harry casts Expelliarmus three times as many times as Hermione.
  • Ron casts one fewer spell than Hermione.
  • Altogether, they cast 94 spells.
Question : Multiply Hermione’s spell count by 4 and subtract 10. What number do you get?"
